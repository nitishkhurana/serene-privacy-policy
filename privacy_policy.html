Serene Privacy Policy

Android Meditation Timer App

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Your privacy is fully protected. Serene is a completely offline app. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data — ever. This policy explains what that means in practice.

01

Introduction

Serene ("the App") is a meditation timer application for Android devices developed by Nitish. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with your use of the App.

By using Serene, you agree to the practices described in this policy. Because the App operates entirely on your device, your experience is private by design — no accounts, no sign-ins, no cloud connectivity required.

02

Information We Collect

We collect absolutely no information

Serene does not collect, access, or process any personal information. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • No names, email addresses, or contact details
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No usage statistics or analytics
  • No crash reports transmitted externally
  • No cookies or tracking technologies
03

How We Use Information

Because we collect no information, we have nothing to use, process, or analyze. All app data — including your meditation sessions, streaks, and preferences — exists solely on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

The App's features function as follows:

  • Meditation sessions — timers run locally; no session data leaves your device
  • Streaks & history — stored only in your device's local app storage
  • Daily reminders — delivered as local Android notifications; no server is contacted
04

Data Storage & Security

All data generated by the App (session history, streak counts, reminder settings) is stored exclusively in your device's local storage using Android's standard app data mechanisms.

This data is sandboxed to the App on your device and is automatically removed when you uninstall Serene. We have no access to it whatsoever.

05

Third-Party Services

Serene does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or crash-reporting services. There are no external services, APIs, or libraries that receive any information from or about you.

The App does not contain advertisements of any kind.

06

Children's Privacy

Serene is suitable for users of all ages. Because the App collects no personal information from anyone, it inherently complies with children's privacy requirements, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar international regulations.

We do not knowingly or unknowingly collect data from children or adults.

07

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the revised policy will be posted at this location with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of the page.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of Serene after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
